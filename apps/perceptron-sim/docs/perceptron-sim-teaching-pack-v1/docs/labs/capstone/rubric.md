# Capstone Rubric (Labs 21–24)

| Criterion | Excellent (3) | Good (2) | Needs work (1) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Correctness** | Metrics and procedures are consistent, τ choice justified | Minor inconsistencies, justification mostly sound | Significant mismatch or unjustified choices |
| **Clarity** | Narrative is concise, visuals labeled, tables readable | Some verbosity or missing labels | Hard to follow; unlabeled visuals |
| **Reproducibility** | Seed & params logged; steps are sufficient to repeat | Some steps missing but likely reproducible | Not reproducible from description |
| **Insight** | Trade-offs and trends explained; limitations noted | Some interpretation present | Little or no interpretation |

**Score:** sum of criteria (max 12). Target ≥ 9.
